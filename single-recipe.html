Sweet Rice Pudding

Want to enjoy Sweet Rice Pudding with family? Here is an easy sweet rice recipe with step by step instructions! If you love to enjoy sweet rice with savoury curries, you have to try this easy sweet rice recipe. Also known as Meethe Chawal in Hindi, sweet rice is a delicious dish from North India that is enjoyed in the entire country. You can also pack Meethe chawal or sweet rice for lunch and enjoy it with or without dry fruits. Have leftover rice from yesterday? Fashion it into a yummy dish with this amazing and easy sweet rice recipe! You won't be disappointed by the end result!

Prep time

20 mins.

Cook time

5 mins.

Servings

6 Servings

Tags: Burger Biryani Momos Sweet Rice Pudding

Instructions

Step 1

Soak & Parboil The Rice. To prepare this lunch recipe, in a medium-sized bowl, soak rice in water for 20 minutes. After that, transfer the rice to the rice cooker and add 2 cups of water to it. Turn on the rice cooker and let these cook. Alternatively, you can parboil the rice over medium flame in a deep bottomed pan with 4 cups of water. Once done drain the excess water and transfer the rice to a bowl.

Step 2

Saute Whole Spices In Ghee. Now, place a large pan on medium flame and add ghee to it. Once the ghee is hot, add green cardamoms, cinnamon stick, turmeric powder and sugar. Fry these well and then switch the flame to low.

Step 3

Cook The Rice & Garnish. Check for the rice now. Once cooked, add rice into the same pan and give it a good stir for a minimum of five minutes on medium flame. Your sweet rice is now prepared. Transfer it to a separate plate and garnish with cashew nuts and dry rose petals before serving.

Ingredients

2 Cup Basmati Rice
2 Cinnamon Stick
2 Green Cardamom
6 Tablespoon Ghee
1/2 Teaspoon Salt
1 Cup Sugar
3 Cup Milk

For Seasoning: 3 Teaspoon Turmeric

For Garnishing: 1 Cup Cashews Dry Rose Petals as required

Tools

Hand Blender

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
